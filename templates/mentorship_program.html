Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our mentorship program

After payment, what's the next process?

After successful payment, our support team will assign you to a WhatsApp group where all further processes take place, including mentorship scheduling and resource access.

Is there any hidden charges?

No, there is only one time payment. All features, bonuses, and support are included in the program fee.

Is this mentorship the right fit for me?

Yes. This mentorship is ideal if you are a student, fresher, or job seeker who wants clear guidance, interview preparation, referrals, and real job support.

What kind of roles is this mentorship suitable for?

This mentorship is suitable for IT, Non-IT, freshers, entry-level, and early-career roles. The guidance is customized based on your background and goals.

Do I need a laptop to attend the mentorship?

A laptop is recommended for resume building, mock interviews, and job applications. However, you can attend calls and sessions using a mobile phone if needed.

Will I receive a certificate after completing the mentorship?

Yes. You will receive a mentorship completion certificate after successfully completing the program.

What language will the mentorship be conducted in?

The mentorship will be conducted in Hinglish (Hindi + English) for easy understanding.

I'm facing issues with payment. What should I do?

If you face any payment issues, please contact our support team and we'll help you complete your enrollment smoothly.

Is this amount refundable?

No, after payment the amount is not refundable as we allocate resources and mentorship slots immediately.
